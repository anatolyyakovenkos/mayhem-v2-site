// WHAT IS MAYHEM V2

You've seen the original Mayhem bot. Cool concept, broken execution — fees leaked out, supply got dumped on holders, same story every cycle.

So we rebuilt it. Mayhem V2 is the version it should have been from day one. Two changes to the contract are all it took to flip the math.

// FEES → COIN

Every fee the bot generates is routed straight back into the token. The contract eats its own output. The more the bot runs, the stronger the coin gets.

// BUYS → BURN

Supply purchased by the bot is sent to the burn address. Forever. Permanent deflation on every buy — not a wallet waiting to dump on you.

Bot earns fees → fees buy supply → supply burns → circulating float drops → next round of fees hits a thinner book. It compounds.

No dev tax. No team allocation. No insider unlocks. No "trust me bro" tokenomics. Just a bot that runs, a contract that burns, and a chart that reflects the math.
